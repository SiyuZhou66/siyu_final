Tips

Four tips that you should be aware of during your shooting time in Hong Kong

#1 Drone

According to Hong Kong national aviation authority, the Hong Kong Civil Aviation Department (HKCAD), it is legal to fly your drone in Hong Kong, but please read the drone regulations before flying it.
HKCAD Contact information: uas@cad.gov.hk / +852 2910 6352

#2 Walk in the Crowds

When you shoot in Hong Kong, it is so easy to get a lot character in one frame.The different feature, movement, expression and emotion of everyone is caputurable.
Hold your camera carefully and do not drop it down.
Be respective and don't distrub people too much.

#3 Go To the Top

There are so many hills in Hong Kong provides fantastic wide angle city view.
Be care of boars, which you could easily meet up in the hills.
You probably need to talk bus to reach most of the hills in Hong Kong,
so don't leave too late at night.

#4 Old School Neiborhood

The builds in Hong Kong especially those old ones would deliver you the intense feeling of Hong Kong culture. The chaotic streets, classic sign of restaurants represent Hong Kong in the best way.
Sham Shui Po, Prince Edward, and Sai Ying Pun are recommended.
